Privacy Policy

We only save the minimal personal information we need for the service to function.

To protect your identity, you do not need to create an account in order to use Privacy Pro. Instead, we assign a random ID to your Privacy Pro subscription that we use to authorize your subscription on your devices. This random ID is not connected to any of your VPN data traffic or credit card information. You can optionally add an email address for ease of authorization. We will only use personal information you provide (for example, your email address if you choose to provide it) to make the service function, including for customer support and sending service notices like receipts, and for no other purpose. We do not sell any personal information.

VPN

We don't keep logs of your VPN activity.

We have no way to tie what you do while connected to the DuckDuckGo VPN to you as an individual.

We only keep anonymous performance metrics that we cannot connect to your VPN activity.

Our VPN servers store generic usage and diagnostic data (for example, CPU load and errors), but nothing connected to any individual. We use this non-identifying information for VPN performance, for example, to ensure VPN servers aren't overloaded, under attack, or having network issues.

We only use dedicated VPN servers.

This means our VPN servers are not shared with anyone else, such as other businesses or service providers. We rent our VPN servers from hosting providers carefully selected to meet our privacy requirements. Our VPN servers are also physically separated from other DuckDuckGo servers, like those used for search infrastructure.

Personal Information Removal

When you enter personal information to have us remove it from data broker sites, that data is only saved on your device, not on our servers.

For example, your name, age and address are all stored locally on your device. If we find additional related information on data broker sites, like alternative names, or addresses, or the names of your relatives, this information is also only stored locally on your device, not on our servers.

The service submits removal requests to data broker sites on your behalf directly from your device, not from our servers.

This is unlike most other services where removal requests are initiated on remote servers. The only personal information we may receive in this process are confirmation emails from data broker sites, which we automatically handle for you and then delete within 72 hours. We regularly re-scan data broker sites to check the removal status of your information. If it has reappeared, we resubmit the removal request.

Identity Theft Restoration

We partner with Iris® Powered by Generali, to provide Identity Theft Restoration.

We do not save any additional personal information when you use Identity Theft Restoration, nor do we share any personal information with Iris. In the event of identity theft, follow the instructions here to connect with Iris directly.
