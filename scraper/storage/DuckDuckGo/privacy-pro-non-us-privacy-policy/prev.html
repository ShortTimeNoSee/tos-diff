Privacy Policy

We only save the minimal personal information we need for the service to function.

To protect your identity, you do not need to create an account in order to use Privacy Pro. Instead, we assign a random ID to your Privacy Pro subscription that we use to authorize your subscription on your devices. This random ID is considered personal information under some privacy laws, but this random ID is not connected to any of your VPN traffic data or credit card information. You can optionally add an email address for ease of authorization. We will only use personal information you provide (for example, your email address if you choose to provide it) to make the service function, including for customer support and sending service notices like receipts, and for no other purpose. Our legal basis for using your personal information in this manner is to provide Privacy Pro to you, that is performance of this contract and other legitimate interests like responding to any customer service issues you raise. We do not sell any personal information.

VPN

We don't keep logs of your VPN activity.

We have no way to tie what you do while connected to the DuckDuckGo VPN to you as an individual.

We only keep anonymous performance metrics that we cannot connect to your VPN activity.

Our VPN servers store generic usage and diagnostic data (for example, CPU load and errors), but nothing connected to any individual. We use this non-identifying information for VPN performance, for example, to ensure VPN servers aren't overloaded, under attack, or having network issues.

We only use dedicated VPN servers.

This means our VPN servers are not shared with anyone else, such as other businesses or service providers. We rent our VPN servers from hosting providers carefully selected to meet our privacy requirements. Our VPN servers are also physically separated from other DuckDuckGo servers, like those used for search infrastructure.
