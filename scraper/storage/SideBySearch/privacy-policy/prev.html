Overview

SideBySearch ("we", "us", "our") provides a service that compares Bing and Google search results. We prioritize privacy and do not store personal data on our servers. All searches are processed in real time.

Information We Process

Search Queries: Your queries are processed in real time solely to fetch search results. We do not log or retain your queries on our servers.

Local Data: Recent search terms are stored locally in your browser using local storage. This data remains on your device and is never transmitted to us.

Cookies & Local Storage:
• We store recent searches in your browser's localStorage under the key recentSearches; we do not set first-party analytics cookies.
• Two first-party cookies (FCCDCF and FCNEC) are set on .sidebysearch.org (path "/") for Cloudflare compliance: they hold encoded state and do not track you across sites.
• If ads load, third-party cookies from Google AdSense may appear (e.g. IDE, test_cookie, ANID, etc.).
You can clear cookies or local storage at any time via your browser's settings.

Third-Party Services

Search APIs: We retrieve search results using Bing Search APIs and Google Custom Search APIs. Data transmitted to these services is subject to their own privacy policies.

Advertising: Our site displays Google Ads to cover API costs. Any data collected through these ads is governed by Google's privacy policy.

Cloudflare Workers: We employ Cloudflare Workers to cache API responses for up to 300 seconds. This caching improves performance and does not store any personal data.

Data Security

We enforce secure connections (HTTPS) and adhere to industry-standard security practices to protect data. No personally identifiable information is stored or processed on our servers.

Additionally, our API endpoints implement rate limiting and access controls to allow only requests originating from our domain. These measures help protect our API resources from abuse.

Referrer Policy

We send Referrer-Policy: no-referrer headers to prevent sharing your page's URL with any third party.

Your Rights and Controls

Local Data: You may clear your recent search data by managing your browser's local storage.

Cookies: Your browser settings allow you to manage or delete cookies at any time.

For any concerns or questions about our data practices, please contact us using the information provided below.
